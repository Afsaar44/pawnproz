Chess is one of the most popular board games that is loved by many people. However, unlike other board games, it is not easy to play. You need to learn the rules and tricks to master the art of playing chess. The game of chess has a long-standing history. It originated in 6th century A.D. in Northwest India. It is also known as Shatranj. In 15th century, it evolved to its current form i.e., Modern Chess. Today, the game is played in over 170 countries.
